About Us

The Spark team, initiated by shenmo, is dedicated to enriching the Linux ecosystem and has formed the Spark Store. We are a young team, most of our members are post-00s, from elementary school students, middle school students, college students, from China.

Despite our different ages and qualifications, our goal of building Linux ecology is the same, and we have the confidence and courage to move forward towards our common goal!
